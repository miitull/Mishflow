Tips
Open this app first
Before Instagram. Before TikTok. Before anything. The moment you get home — open this. That one second is the entire habit.
The 2-minute rule
Don't think about finishing the workout. Just start for 2 minutes. Put the bands on. That's it. You'll almost always keep going — starting is the only hard part.
Schedule scroll time
Add "phone time 30 min" as an actual task at 10:30pm. Knowing it's coming makes it easier to delay it. Unscheduled scrolling turns into 2 hours gone.
3 tasks max
You have 5 hours. Don't add 8 tasks. 3 real ones done beats 8 abandoned ones every time. The AI will tell you if you're overloading.
Save to your iPhone home screen
Open this page in Safari → tap the Share button (box with arrow) → "Add to Home Screen" → Done. It will look and work like a real app on your phone.
