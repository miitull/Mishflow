Install on your iPhone
Open this page in Safari → tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen". MISHFLOW lives on your home screen like a real app.
Open this before anything else
Before Instagram. Before TikTok. The moment you get home — open MISHFLOW. That one second is the entire habit.
The 2-minute rule
Don't commit to 45 minutes of exercise. Just put your bands on. Start for 2 minutes. You'll almost always keep going.
Schedule your scroll time
Add "phone time" as an actual task at 10:30pm. Knowing it's coming makes it easy to delay it. Unscheduled scrolling becomes 2 hours gone.
3 tasks max per night
You have 5 hours. Don't add 8 tasks. 3 real ones done beats 8 abandoned ones every single time. Be ruthlessly realistic.
